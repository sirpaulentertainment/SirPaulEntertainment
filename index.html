About Me

I’m SirPaul, a video–first creator obsessed with rhythm, emotion, and detail. I don’t just make content — I shape moments into stories people remember.

Over the last few years I’ve edited for brands, creators, and startups that care about how they show up online. From quick-fire UGC to cinematic cuts, I focus on three things: clarity, emotion, and conversion.

I understand both sides of the game: the creative that stops the scroll, and the strategy that keeps people watching. Every frame has a job. Every cut has a reason.

If you’re looking for someone who simply follows instructions, I’m not your guy. If you want someone who treats your idea like their own and thinks in hooks, pacing, and outcomes — that’s where I come in.
